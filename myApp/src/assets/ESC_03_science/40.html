The digestive system.
The digestion is a process in which our body uses the
food to transform it in nutrients.
The food we eat is the fuel we need to get our energy
and there is where the function of this system start.
The food gets decomposed and reduced to nutrients.
The nutrients are the ones our body uses. The rest of
the food gets wasted or eliminated.
The food passes through the esophagus to the
stomach. The gastric ﬂuids are in the stomach and
they are in charge of transforming the food into mass,
then the mass goes to the small intestine, where the
nutrients get absorbed and go into the bloodstream,
that’s the way nutrients get
carried to the rest of the
body.
The rest of the food mass passes to the large intestine
where they are eliminated as excrement.
