The eat well plate
Here are ﬁve easy tips to follow for healthier children.
Eat a balanced diet – see the eatwell plate diagram to
explain the food pyramid. Key principles are:
Eat plenty of wholegrain varieties of bread, rice,
pasta.
Eat plenty of fruit and vegetables, at least ﬁve
portions a day
Eat lower fat diary where ever possible
Meat, ﬁsh, eggs, beans are good sources of protein,
choose the lower fat version.
Minimize the amount of foods and drinks high in
sugar or fat. The eat well plate above shows how
much to eat of the diﬀerent food groups. It is also
recommended to drink natural water every time we
eat.
