Human beings communicate themselves by using
words and sounds. When the sound is music, it
entertains, relaxes, gets you happy, etc.
Sound waves require a medium in which to travel.
Sound waves are characterized by the generic
properties of waves, which are: tone, frequency,
wavelength, period, amplitude, intensity, speed,
and direction (sometimes speed and direction are
combined as a velocity vector, or wavelength and
direction are combined as a wave vector).
Tone:
High or low sound.
Intensity:
Strong, weak or soft.
Duration:
Long or short.
Timbre:
The hearing sense distinguishes who or what
made the noise.
