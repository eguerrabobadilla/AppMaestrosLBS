Draw in your notebook the movement of
the moon, add a photograph and write
the time it takes to do these movements
around the Earth.
The moon has two movements: rotation and
revolution.
Rotation is the movement that the moon makes on
its own axis. This movement happens in 29 days.
Revolution is the movement that the moon makes
around the Earth. This movement happens in 29 days
and 12 hrs.
