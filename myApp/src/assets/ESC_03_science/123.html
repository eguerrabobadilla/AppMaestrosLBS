Homogeneous and
heterogeneous.
Mixtures are combinations of diﬀerent substances. These
mixtures can be homogeneous or heterogeneous.
A homogeneous is a mixture that has substances combined
and dissolved totally.
A heterogeneous mixture is when the substances are not
completely combined and can be spread by the use of a
procedure.
